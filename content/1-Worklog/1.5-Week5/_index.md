### Week 5 Objectives:

* Learn the fundamentals of containerization on AWS.
* Understand how Amazon ECR stores and manages container images.
* Explore Amazon ECS and its components: tasks, services, clusters.
* Deploy a containerized application using ECS Fargate (serverless compute).
* Strengthen skills in modern cloud-native application deployment.

* Built a strong foundation in Docker containerization and the process of building and tagging images.
