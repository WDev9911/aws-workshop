### Week 9 Objectives:

* Learn relational database services on AWS.
* Understand Amazon RDS architecture, backups, Multi-AZ, and maintenance.
* Explore Amazon Aurora and its performance/scalability benefits.
* Learn how to connect applications to RDS and manage database security.
* Deploy, configure, and test a real RDS database instance.

* Understood how Amazon RDS provides managed relational database services.
